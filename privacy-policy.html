Privacy Policy - PrizeGold

Effective Date: January 4, 2026

Overview

PrizeGold is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our gold investment tracking application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Gold investment portfolio data (purchase prices, quantities, dates)
  • Price alert preferences and notification settings
  • User preference settings (units, currency, notification timing)
  • Historical price data cache for improved performance

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT require user registration or accounts
  • We do NOT store any data on external servers
  • We do NOT track user behavior or analytics
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

Third-Party Data Sources

Gold Price API

  • We retrieve real-time gold price data from metalpriceapi.com
  • This service provides current gold prices and historical data
  • No personal information is shared with this service
  • API calls are made anonymously

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • ALL your data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
  • Your investment portfolio and preferences never leave your device
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used

Data Backup

  • Your data may be included in your device's iCloud backup (if enabled)
  • This is managed entirely by Apple's secure backup system
  • We have no access to your backed-up data

No In-App Purchases or Advertising

Premium Application

  • PrizeGold contains NO in-app purchases
  • NO advertising or third-party promotional content
  • NO data monetization or sharing with advertisers

Your Rights and Control

Data Control

  • You have complete control over your data
  • Delete the app to permanently remove all stored data
  • No account deletion process needed as no accounts are created
  • Your data cannot be recovered once the app is deleted

Notifications

  • Price alerts are processed locally on your device
  • You can enable/disable notifications at any time in Settings
  • Notification preferences are stored locally only

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and no personal information is collected, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
